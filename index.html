An AI Academy, systematised — and shipped as a product. CapabilityOS encodes how an organisation builds AI capability role by role: what good, safe AI use looks like for each role, how people ramp over 30/60/90 days, and how that use is governed, assessed and measured. I designed the methodology and built the platform end to end.

  • AI adoption governance — every role gets an AI Adoption Playbook: approved uses, risky uses, human-review checkpoints and checklists.
  • Capability defined by role — observable, evidence-based expectations per capability area, so "good AI use" is assessable, not a slogan.
  • 30/60/90-day ramp pathways — phased plans with actions, assignments and explicit AI-usage rules for a person's first ninety days.
  • Assessment & manager enablement — questions with scoring rubrics, plus manager coaching guides: observation points, questions, red flags.
  • Measurable uplift — per-organisation capability dashboards make progress visible and reportable to leadership.
  • And I built it — full-stack: Next.js 15, multi-tenant Supabase (Postgres + row-level security), an LLM engine with structured output, Stripe billing.
